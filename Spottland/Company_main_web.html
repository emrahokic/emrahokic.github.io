Team

Orhan Roksa

Co-Founder & President

Born in Mostar, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Orhan Roksa is a junior Mechanical Engineering at the University of Oklahoma and co-founder of Spottland. He is a United World College Scholar, Rita H. Lottinville Scholar and a winner of other numerous prestigious awards and recognitions. Orhan is currently involved in Biomechanical Testing for Soft Biological Tissues and Synthetic Biomaterials and leading engineer in designing one of the first full-scale wind tunnels for testing UAS propeller performances and the University’s facilities.

orhan@spottland.com

Marko Mestrovic

Co-Founder & CEO

Marko Mestrovic from Prozor-Rama, Bosnia and Herzegovina is CEO and co-founder of SpottLand LLC with responsibility for business planning, enterprise, marketing, research and development. He is also junior Mechanical Engineering and Davis Scholar at the University of Oklahoma. Marko describes himself as passionate, dedicated, trustworthy young person with a vision, living his life as a verb – thinking, learning, and creating.

marko@spottland.com

Abdulkerim Isanovic

Web & Software developer

Abdulkerim is a sophomore IT student. Born in Sarajevo, biggest city and capital of Bosnia and Herzegovina, he always wanted to do great things. He knows his way around HTML, CSS, C++ and C# programming languages. He has great vision on how apps should look like, and does his best to ever improve upon on it. He is also passionate about networking and telecommunications, making his all-rounded skills perfect addition for the team.

kerim@spottland.com
